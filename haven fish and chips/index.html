About Us

The Haven Fish and Chips Restaurant is dedicated to serving the freshest, crispiest fish and chips in town. We take pride in our traditional recipes, quality ingredients, and exceptional service. The owners: Ryan and Stephen are father and son who have now owned this fine fish and chip restaurant for the better part of 2 years now, having no experience with this they decided to jump into the deep end with this (pun intended) but have now started to thrive in this new era of their life and continue to provide the best fish and chips they can!
